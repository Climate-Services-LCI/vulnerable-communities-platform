Climate Data

Physical vulnerability for wells and water systems, ratio of precipitation to population and cultivated land, and climate projections for increased summer temperatures.


Social Data

A bundle of common social indicators relevant across the climate hazards.
Specific indicators that are linked to drought related vulnerability.


Combined Map

Identifies areas with more drought impacts present or projected, and social conditions that amplify the effects of those climate conditions.
