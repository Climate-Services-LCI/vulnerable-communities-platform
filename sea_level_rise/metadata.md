Climate Data

Sea level rise flooding/inundation, shoreline changes and erosion, and groundwater hazards.


Social Data

A bundle of common social indicators relevant across the climate hazards.
Specific indicators that are linked to vulnerability to SLR and related coastal concerns. 


Combined Map

Identifies areas with more SLR and coastal impacts present or projected, and social conditions that amplify the effects of those climate conditions.
